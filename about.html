About Lexa

I have been crafting since I was a young age, both of my grandmas taught me how to sew, and one taught me how to sew and crochet. I have loved creating and having a creative outlet, and have finally made the decision to sell my work. Under my etsy shop, you can find mainly crochet pieces with a few artworks and hopefully some 3D printed items soon! The link to my etsy shop is posted above.
